Bio: Jennifer Kabat’s *The Eighth Moon* on a 1840s socialist uprising in her town will be published by Milkweed Editions in Spring 2024. Half of a diptych, the second volume *Nightshining* will come out in 2025. Her work has been supported by numerous grants including a Silvers Foundation Grant and a Warhol Foundation Arts Writers Grant for her criticism. Her essays have appeared in *BOMB*, *Granta*, *Frieze*, *McSweeney’s*, *The Believer*, *Virginia Quarterly Review*, *LARB*, *New York Review*, *4 Columns* and the *White Review* and been anthologized in *Best American Essays*. She often collaborates with artists and contributes to museum catalogues. An apprentice herbalist, she lives in rural upstate New York and serves on her volunteer fire department.
