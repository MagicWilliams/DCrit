Title: Kat de Haan

----

Role: Design researcher and strategist

----

Bio:

Kat de Haan is a design researcher and strategist who has worked in both digital and physical product, across the innovation cycle. Her interest is cyborg anthropology, and in approaching design problems through interrogating relationships between human / non-human entities. 

She holds a B.A. in Anthropology from the University of British Columbia. She has worked in both agency and in-house settings, with clients including Whistler Blackcomb, OXO, Braun, Revlon, Milwaukee Tool, Bloomberg and Roche. She is an avid bird watcher.
