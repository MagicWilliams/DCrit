Role: Playwright, screenwriter, content creator

Bio:

Scarlett is a playwright, screenwriter, and content creator living in New York City. The daughter of a designer, she is particularly interested in what design trends can tell us about culture and its values. She currently leads content strategy and storytelling for PepsiCo Design + Innovation.
 
Her writing and projects have been featured in Time, MSNBC, and Alma. She holds a B.F.A. from New York University, where she double majored in Dramatic Writing and Social and Cultural Analysis.
