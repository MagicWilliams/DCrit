Title: Lisa Hardon

----

Role: Design curator, writer, and project manager

----

Bio: Lisa Hardon is a design curator, writer and project manager based in Amsterdam. She has been working in the design field for 10 years, for a diverse range of clients such as Dutch Design Awards, Dutch Design Week, Van Eesteren Museum, and Elle Decoration. She likes telling bigger stories about the world through design to show people the beauty of mundane, everyday items.
